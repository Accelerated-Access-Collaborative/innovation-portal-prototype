Information for organisations

Accessing the data in Health Connect

Organisations that meet the criteria below may apply for access to the data in Health Connect. The application will be reviewed and a decision made by the Health Connect oversight and governance board.

Organisations must be publicly funded and operate on a national level with a legitimate and clearly stated purpose for:

  1. providing health and social care systems (or part of health and social care systems) who are requiring information on Health Technologies, which are available on a national level for the purpose of developing guidance, advice and policies, OR
  2. commissioning or procuring health technologies, OR
  3. supporting development or adoption of Health Technologies,

Organisations must also:

  1. be committed to actively engaging with the Health Connect system and participate in agreed collaborative and engagement processes as required by NHSE and/or the Health Connect Team from time to time;
  2. be able to demonstrate relevant expertise within the Accessor Organisation and/or Data Accessor team relevant to the specific purpose for using Health Connect;
  3. have skills and ability to incorporate information into a fair, accurate and balanced way;
  4. have policies and processes in place for handling Confidential Information.
