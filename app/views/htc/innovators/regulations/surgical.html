Surgical procedures and techniques Regulations

{{ contentsList({ items: [ { href: "/htc/innovators/regulations", text: "Medicines and medical devices" }, { href: "/htc/innovators/regulations/digital", text: "Digital products, services and components" }, { href: "/htc/innovators/regulations/surgical", text: "Surgical procedures and techniques", current: "true" }, { href: "/htc/innovators/regulations/other", text: "Models of care and other innovations" } ] }) }}

Innovative surgical procedures and techniques do not have specific regulatory approval processes, though any medical products and devices that are used may require separate approvals.

The Royal College of Surgeons of England have produced a best-practice guide to surgical innovation which is applicable to the whole of the UK.
